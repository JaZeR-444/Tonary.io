Brand System · v2.0

Obsidian Orchid

A lavender-on-obsidian identity for a premium creative & music-tech interface — dark, calm, softly technical, and drawn directly from the icon's violet palette.

Obsidian

White

Primary usage. Violet leads every brand moment. Surfaces stay near-black with a faint indigo tint. Cyan appears only as a quiet technical highlight; green / amber / rose are reserved strictly for success / warning / error. Let contrast, spacing, and the mark do the work — never more than one loud accent per view.
